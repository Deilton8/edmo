About EDMO Africa Trading

EDMO Africa Trading Pty Ltd was founded by a team of experienced professionals who saw the potential for better, more efficient cross-border trade between Southern Africa and Mozambique.

Our Vision

To be Africa's most trusted partner in cross-border trade, creating seamless connections that power sustainable growth across the continent.

Our Mission

To redefine procurement and logistics in Southern Africa through innovative, reliable cross-border solutions.

  • Bridge logistical gaps between businesses and opportunities
  • Leverage advanced systems for seamless operations
  • Empower clients with tailored supply chain strategies
  • Foster economic development across the region
