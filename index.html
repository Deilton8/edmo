About EDMO Africa Trading

EDMO Africa Trading Pty Ltd was founded by a team of experienced professionals who saw the potential for better, more efficient cross-border trade between Southern Africa and Mozambique. What began as a small family initiative focused on solving procurement and logistics challenges quickly grew into a trusted partner for businesses across the region. From humble beginnings, we’ve expanded our services across all provinces, dedicated to delivering reliable and cost-effective solutions. Our mission is to simplify the supply chain, bridge logistical gaps, and ensure that goods move seamlessly, helping businesses thrive across Africa.
